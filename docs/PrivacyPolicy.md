# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** April 2026

## 1. Information We Collect
When you use ReseolabX, we may collect the following types of information:
- **License Information:** To verify your access and prevent piracy, we collect information related to your license key, your email address used during activation, and a hardware identifier associated with your device.
- **Application Data:** All your project data, documents, references, and graphs are stored **locally** on your device. We do not transmit or store your research data on our servers.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
We use the collected information solely for:
- Activating and validating your software license.
- Providing customer support if you encounter licensing issues.

## 3. Data Security
We implement strict security measures to protect your license data. Since your research data remains local to your device, its security depends on your local machine's security practices.

## 4. Third-Party Services
We do not share your license information or any local data with third parties, except as necessary to process payments or comply with legal obligations.

## 5. Contact Us
If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact our support team.
